# 11.2 Image Galleries, Part 2

If you want to add an image gallery to your WordPress site and need more functionality than the _Gallery_ block provides then, as you probably could have guessed, there's a plugin for that! Lots of them, in fact.

1. From the _Admin area_ of your _Tutorial Site_, go to _Plugins > Add New Plugin_.
2. Search for _FooGallery_ and install and activate the plugin that shows up as _Gallery by FooGallery_ by _FooPlugins_.
3. Click _FooGallery_ on the _Admin menu_.
4. Click _Add New Gallery_.
5. Type _Our Hostel Accommodations_ in the _Add title_ box.
6. Select _Responsive_ as the _Gallery Layout_.
7. Click _Add Media_.
8. Use _Instant Images_ to find some photos that look like a hostel.
9.  Choose the photos that you imported and click _Select_.
10. You can add additional photos by clicking _Add Media_ again and repeating steps 8 and 9. You can also add photos that you have already uploaded to your media library by clicking _Media Library_ instead of _Instant Images_ in the _Add Media_ window.
11. After you have added all the photos you want, you can rearrange the photos by dragging and dropping them into the order you want them to appear in the gallery. You can also click the _i_ icon that appears when you hover over a photo to edit the photo's details, including the title, caption, alt text, and description. And you click the _X_ icon to remove a photo from the gallery.
12. Under _Gallery Settings_, explore the various settings available. For example, you can change the number of columns in the mobile version of thegallery, the spacing between photos, and the size of the thumbnails.
13. Click _Publish_.
14. Edit the _Accommodations_ page.
15. Add the _FooGallery_ block to the page and select the _Our Hostel Accommodations_ gallery you just created from the dropdown menu in the block settings.

Take a look at the gallery. Browse the photos and click on them to see how the lightbox works. You may even want to try out some of the different settings for the gallery and see how they change the way the gallery looks and functions.

Of course, there are tons of other plugins. I encourage you to check out some other image gallery plugins and see what you can find!
