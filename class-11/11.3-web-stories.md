# 11.3 Web Stories

If you use _Google Search_ and/or _Google Discover_ on a mobile device, you probably have seen [_Web Stories_](https://creators.google/en-us/content-creation-products/own-your-content/web-stories/). If you haven't, the concept is similar to _Instagram Stories_, except that _Web Stories_ use only standard Web technologies.

_Web Stories_ are full-screen, visually rich, tap-through content pieces optimized for mobile devices. They feature text, images, video, and animations. These stories are discoverable in Google Search and can increase traffic and engagement on your site since they actually live on your site.

Feel free to check out the link to understand more, or keep going in this tutorial and all should become clear soon!

## Installing the Web Stories Plugin

1. Log into your WordPress Dashboard.
2. Go to **Plugins > Add New**.
3. Search for **"Web Stories"**.
4. Find the plugin by **Google** and click **Install Now**.
5. Click **Activate**.

## Creating a Web Story

1. Go to **Stories > Add New**.
2. Use the visual Web Stories editor.
   - **Canvas** (center): page preview.
   - **Pages** (bottom left): manage pages.
   - **Media Library** (left panel): images and video.
   - **Design Tools** (right panel): position, text, colors, etc.

### Build Your Story

- Add a background image or video.
- Insert short, clear text within the safe zones.
- Add more pages with the **+** icon.
- Use templates to speed up design (optional).
- Below is an outline for a sample story you can create to practice with the plugin. You can use your own creativity to make it your own!

## Publishing Your Story

- Fill in the **Document tab**:
  - Title
  - Cover Image
  - Excerpt (optional)
  - Permalink (optional)
- Click **Publish**, **Preview**, or **Save Draft**.

### Embedding Your Story

1. Edit a post or page.
2. Use the **Web Stories block**.
3. Select your story.

## Best Practices

- Keep it short: 5–10 slides.
- Focus on visuals.
- Use video where possible.
- One link per page max.
- Ensure accessibility (alt text, readable fonts).
- Use high-quality vertical media (1080x1920 recommended).

## Summary
The Web Stories plugin makes it easy to build engaging, SEO-friendly visual content. It's great for boosting user engagement and enhancing your WordPress content strategy.

## **Web Story Outline: “Escape to Geekaway”**

Let's build a Web Story designed to inspire interest and encourage clicks or bookings.

---

### **Page 1 – Title Page**

- **Headline**: _Welcome to Geekaway_
- **Subheading**: _The ultimate resort for tech lovers and their favorite people_
- **Image suggestion**: A sunny, modern resort exterior with subtle tech-themed decor (e.g., solar panels, digital signage)

---

### **Page 2 – What is Geekaway?**

- **Headline**: _Where Geeks Vacation in Style_
- **Body**: _Cutting-edge tech. Nerdy adventures. And space for everyone._
- **Image suggestion**: Split-screen image: left side with guests in VR headsets, right side with people relaxing by the pool.

---

### **Page 3 – Activities for Geeks**

- **Headline**: _Hackathons. Game Tournaments. Drone Racing._
- **Body**: _Level up your fun with hands-on workshops, escape rooms, and more._
- **Image suggestion**: Enthusiasts around computers, someone flying a drone, or an esports-style gaming room.

---

### **Page 4 – Activities for Normies**

- **Headline**: _Relax, Recharge, Repeat._
- **Body**: _Spa treatments, art classes, yoga on the beach—zero tech required._
- **Image suggestion**: A serene spa setting, yoga on the sand, or a painting class in progress.

---

### **Page 5 – Family & Couples Friendly**

- **Headline**: _Everyone Belongs Here_
- **Body**: _Whether you're bonding over tech or taking a break from it—we've got you covered._
- **Image suggestion**: Families enjoying different areas of the resort, couples enjoying both high-tech and low-tech activities.

---

### **Page 6 – Special Events**

- **Headline**: _Theme Weeks & Guest Speakers_
- **Body**: _From sci-fi cosplay cons to keynote talks by tech leaders—there's always something new._
- **Image suggestion**: A panel with a tech speaker, costumed guests, or a night-time light show.

---

### **Page 7 – Plan Your Geekaway**

- **Headline**: _Book Your Adventure Today_
- **CTA**: _Tap to explore packages and availability._
- **Image suggestion**: A vibrant collage of resort scenes with a bold “Tap to Book” or arrow icon.
